COINNECT Mobile Design

COINNECT is a mobile application designed to facilitate collaborative saving among friends and family. Its design focuses on simplicity and visual clarity to provide an intuitive experience from the first use. Navigation is smooth and straightforward, with large buttons, recognizable icons, and a well-defined hierarchical structure that allows users to create savings goals, invite friends, record contributions, and track progress easily. Additionally, it incorporates visual elements such as progress bars and reminders to keep users motivated. The entire design was based on principles of accessibility and usability to meet the needs of a broad audience.

App Goal: The main goal of COINNECT is to provide a platform that allows groups of friends and families to save collaboratively to achieve common goals, offering features such as contribution tracking, milestone setting, and automated reminders, and measuring its effectiveness through the number of successfully achieved goals and the total amount saved within the application.

Role: I was the lead UX/UI designer for the Xchange app. I was responsible for designing the user flow, creating wireframes, developing the visual interface, and ensuring accessibility and usability across all screens.

Technologies: Figma

COINNECT Web Design

The web version of COINNECT retains the essence of its mobile version, prioritizing a clear, organized, and accessible user experience. The design focuses on a clean visual architecture, with a strategic distribution of elements to facilitate navigation on larger screens. A color palette consistent with the app's identity was used, highlighting action buttons and key sections such as savings goals, contributions, and group activities. The interface allows users to create, view, and manage goals collaboratively, as well as manage friends and payment methods. Additionally, the design was adapted to ensure compatibility with different resolutions and devices, ensuring a smooth and responsive experience for all users.

Xchange

Xchange is a mobile app designed to simplify currency conversion for international travelers and online shoppers. It allows users to register, select their country or region, and instantly convert currencies using real-time exchange rates in a clean, ad-free interface.

Role: I was the lead UX/UI designer for the Xchange app. I was responsible for designing the user flow, creating wireframes, developing the visual interface, and ensuring accessibility and usability across all screens.
